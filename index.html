Mission Statement

Housemates is a platform with which roommates can connect quickly and concisely. Using the features implemented on the webpage, roommates can easily reference a shared calendar, view a consistent shopping list, effectively divide chores and tasks, and easily change landlord or contact information for the entire household.

The first roommate to sign up using Housemates is given a unique household ID that is used to let other people join their house and access shared information. Housemates are able to keep a collaborative grocery shopping and chores list that helps keep individuals accountable for their actions as a Housemate. Housemates.fun provides a central website for roommates to use that will improve time management, communication, and relationships.
